Electric Locomotives

Diesel locomotives revolutionized Indian Railways, with workhorses like the WDM-2, WDP-4, and WDG-4 powering both passenger and freight services. While electric traction is replacing them in many regions, diesel engines remain crucial for remote routes, heavy haulage, and strategic operations across the country. Work in Progress
