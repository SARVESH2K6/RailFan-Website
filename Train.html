Trains

The Trains highlights the diverse range of Indian Railways, from the luxurious Rajdhani and Shatabdi Express to the futuristic Vande Bharat and RRTS. Explore the evolution of passenger and freight trains that connect the nation with speed, comfort, and efficiency.

Vande Bharat Express

India’s first indigenously built semi-high-speed train, known for its sleek design, modern amenities, and rapid acceleration, revolutionizing rail travel.

Gatimaan Express

India’s first semi-high-speed train, offering luxurious seating, modern amenities, and a top speed of 160 km/h, making it the fastest train in the country.

Rajdhani Express

India's premier long-distance train, offering high-speed connectivity, air-conditioned coaches, and complimentary meals, linking the capital with major cities.

Shatabdi Express

Premium daytime intercity train known for its high speed, limited stops, and comfortable seating, connecting major cities with efficiency.

Tejas Express

India’s first semi-high-speed fully air-conditioned train, offering modern amenities, onboard catering, and premium comfort for a faster and luxurious travel experience.

Duronto Express

Premium long-distance train in India, known for its non-stop service between major cities, offering speed, comfort, and fewer halts than other express trains.

Humsafar Express

Premium fully air-conditioned train designed for long-distance travel, featuring modern amenities, bio-toilets, CCTV surveillance, and enhanced passenger comfort.

Garib Rath Express

A premium economy train offering affordable travel with air-conditioned coaches, designed to provide budget-friendly comfort for passengers traveling long distances.

Double-Decker Express

Unique bi-level train offering high seating capacity, air-conditioned coaches, and a comfortable ride, ideal for busy intercity routes.

Superfast Express

Trains with fewer halts and higher speeds (above 55 km/h).

Mail/Express Trains

Standard long-distance trains with multiple stops.

Jan Shatabdi Express

A budget version of the Shatabdi Express with both AC and non-AC coaches.

Intercity Express

Connects major cities within a state or neighboring states.

MEMU (indexline Electric Multiple Unit)

Short-distance commuter trains with electric traction.

DEMU (Diesel Electric Multiple Unit)

Diesel-powered short-distance trains.

Passenger Trains

Slow-moving trains stopping at every station.

Antyodaya Express

A fully unreserved train service aimed at providing affordable and comfortable travel for the economically weaker sections of society, with modern amenities.

Palace on Wheels

India’s first luxury train, offering a royal travel experience through Rajasthan, featuring opulent interiors and heritage excursions.

Deccan Odyssey

A luxury train that showcases the rich culture of Maharashtra and beyond, with world-class amenities and curated experiences.

Maharajas' Express

India’s most luxurious train, providing a royal journey with five-star hospitality, covering iconic heritage destinations.

Golden Chariot

A luxury train exploring South India’s heritage, wildlife, and beaches, blending regal comfort with cultural discovery.

Royal Rajasthan on Wheels

Luxury train that offers a regal travel experience, showcasing the grandeur of Rajasthan with lavish interiors, fine dining, and heritage excursions.

Kavi Guru Express

Trains dedicated to Rabindranath Tagore's legacy.

Vivek Express

Trains named after Swami Vivekananda, connecting distant locations.

Bharat Gaurav Trains

Theme-based trains for Indian tourism.

Mahaparinirvan Express

Special tourist train that follows the Buddhist Circuit, connecting key pilgrimage sites related to Gautama Buddha’s life and teachings.

Mumbai Local Trains

Lifeline of Mumbai’s public transport.

Chennai Suburban Railway

Local train service in Chennai.

Kolkata Suburban Railway

Local train network in Kolkata.

Vande Metro

Short-distance, high-frequency train designed for urban and suburban travel, offering a fast, modern, and comfortable alternative to conventional local trains.

RRTS

A high-speed urban rail network designed for fast, efficient regional travel, reducing congestion in metro cities.

Container Trains

Special freight trains designed for efficient cargo transport, ensuring fast and economical movement of goods across India.

Goods Trains

Standard freight transport for coal, cement, food grains, etc.
